When Harry met Sally, almost.

Aileen and Manik met in college, at Rutgers University where they woere both undergrads. They quickly discovered that they both grew up just minutes away from each other in New Jersey. They both had the same interests: from the bands they listened to down to the fact that they both always enjoyed having a camera in their hand.

It didn’t take long for them to realize that they were perfect for each other. They both finished college—Aileen graduating from Rutgers with a Bachelors in Finance, and Manik from Kean University, with a Bachelors in Advertising and Graphic Design—and started on the next piece of their journey, together.

When you realize you want to spend the rest of your life with a person,
you want the rest of your life to start as soon as possible.

Paris is always a good idea.

There are few things more beautiful in this world than Paris. In May of 2012, we gave in to our wanderlust and travelled to Paris for a few weeks. We explored the beautiful architecture, took boat rides down the Seinne and watched in awe as the Tour d’Eiffel, standing magnificently, began sparkling and lit up the night sky.

With our love of photography, we are rarely ever in a photograph together. So, we “decided” to hire a professional photographer, Pierre, and go back to a few of their favorite spots around the city.

Little did Aileen know, I had a ring weighing down my pocket for this whole trip.

We arrived at the Champ de Mars at sunset, the second-to-last stop with Pierre. We moved around the Champ de Mars to get different angles of the beautiful space. Once we moved closer to the center of the lawn and as the sun peaked out from behind the trees, Itook our the ring, got down on one knee and asked my best friend to become my partner in life.
